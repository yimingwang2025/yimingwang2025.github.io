Dandy's World

  • Dandy's World is a mascot horror survival multiplayer experience created by BlushCrunch Studio.
  • The game was created on January 26th, 2024, but wasn't publicly available until its Alpha release on June 14th, 2024.
  • Players must extract machines, collect research, earn new toons, and survive deadly "twisteds" that roam around pursuing players.
  • The game was inspired by Bendy and the Ink Machine, Dead by Daylight, and Identity V.
  • Dandy's World reached 1 Billion visits on December 26th, 2024 and 2 Billion visits by February 21st, 2025.
  • The world record for highest floor reached in a Glitchless run is 267 on Version 0.7.2.
  • The most dangerous entity is Twisted Dandy, who can instantly kill a toon regardless of how many hearts they have.
  • The highest number of active players was 638,852 on March 14th, 2025, when the Yatta Update was released.

Scary Sushi

  • Scary Sushi is a horror, cooking-based Roblox game created by Evil Twin Games.
  • Players take part in a group interview for a Chef position at the notorious Moonlight Sushi restaurant.
  • Midway through the interview, a group called the Night Keepers warns that this is no ordinary restaurant and that you must escape.
  • The game features multiple entities including Norman, Oscar, Ivy, Flame, Triplets, Blob, and Kiyoku.
  • Players must cook sushi dishes while avoiding monsters throughout the game.
  • The game has expanded to include Chapter 2, with more chapters planned.
  • There are different game modes including Story mode and Survival mode.
  • Players must find ingredients in special rooms and use cooking stations to prepare dishes.

BlushCrunch Studio

  • BlushCrunch Studio is the development team behind the popular Roblox horror game Dandy's World.
  • The studio was founded in late 2023 by a team of experienced Roblox developers.
  • Before Dandy's World, the team worked on smaller projects that helped them perfect their horror game mechanics.
  • BlushCrunch focuses on creating immersive horror experiences with unique art styles.
  • The name "BlushCrunch" was inspired by the contrast between cute aesthetics and horror elements in their games.
  • The studio is known for its regular content updates and strong community engagement.
  • They often host special in-game events during holidays like Halloween and Christmas.
  • BlushCrunch's development philosophy centers on "horror that's both accessible and challenging."

Evil Twin Games

  • Evil Twin Games is the development studio responsible for creating Scary Sushi.
  • The studio was formed in early 2024 by a duo of Roblox developers who were previously known for their puzzle games.
  • Evil Twin Games gets its name from the theme of duality that runs through their games.
  • The studio pioneered the "horror cooking" sub-genre on Roblox, blending survival horror with cooking mechanics.
  • They're known for their detailed environment design and atmospheric sound design.
  • Evil Twin Games has won several Roblox community awards for innovation in gameplay.
  • The developers regularly interact with fans on social media and incorporate player feedback into updates.
  • Their roadmap for Scary Sushi includes at least five chapters in total, with new cooking mechanics in each.
