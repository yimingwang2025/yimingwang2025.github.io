Dandy's World

  • Dandy's World is a mascot horror survival multiplayer experience created by BlushCrunch Studio.
  • The game was created on January 26th, 2024, but wasn't publicly available until its Alpha release on June 14th, 2024.
  • Players must extract machines, collect research, earn new toons, and survive deadly "twisteds" that roam around pursuing players.
  • The game was inspired by Bendy and the Ink Machine, Dead by Daylight, and Identity V.
  • Dandy's World reached 1 Billion visits on December 26th, 2024 and 2 Billion visits by February 21st, 2025.
  • The world record for highest floor reached in a Glitchless run is 267 on Version 0.7.2.
  • The most dangerous entity is Twisted Dandy, who can instantly kill a toon regardless of how many hearts they have.
  • The highest number of active players was 638,852 on March 14th, 2025, when the Yatta Update was released.

Scary Sushi

  • Scary Sushi is a horror, cooking-based Roblox game created by Evil Twin Games.
  • Players take part in a group interview for a Chef position at the notorious Moonlight Sushi restaurant.
  • Midway through the interview, a group called the Night Keepers warns that this is no ordinary restaurant and that you must escape.
  • The game features multiple entities including Norman, Oscar, Ivy, Flame, Triplets, Blob, and Kiyoku.
  • Players must cook sushi dishes while avoiding monsters throughout the game.
  • The game has expanded to include Chapter 2, with more chapters planned.
  • There are different game modes including Story mode and Survival mode.
  • Players must find ingredients in special rooms and use cooking stations to prepare dishes.
